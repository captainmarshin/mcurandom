Help & About

Hi! 👋
Thank you for visiting MCU Random.
I am a big fan of Marvel Cinematic Universe and like others, who like this Universe, often rewatch all projects.



On one day I couldn't choose what to watch today? I was search in Google fast random from all MCU, but not found anything interesting. So i decide to make a complete randomiser from all MCU projects. It was hard, because i’m not professional web designer, but with Google and tenacity, after a few weeks, I present you: MCU Random - Get random title from Marvel Cinematic Universe! This random take part from 23 movies, 13 TV shows (include episodes), 6 one-shots and 2 web series. And as a bonus you can make random from movies, which not join in MCU, starts from 1998 Blade movie. Over time and the release of new episodes and projects I’ll add this in Random. On site launch moment (and writing this text) last project is The Falcon and The Winter Soldier (Season 1, Episode 6: One World, One People).

Website

MCU Random is very simple site in functionality and code. Here I'll show you how to use it and little user tips.

QuickRandom

On the top right of app you can see Quick Random function. It’s shows you random title from all MCU titles (exclude episodes) in one click. Press on this element and it’s shows you new title. Work like a watch. P.S And here you can find little Easter Egg 🐣

Result Window

Press on Poster image to open current title on IMDb.
Another way to close window is press Esc button on keyboard.

MCU Timeline beta

At the bottom of Random Cards you can see MCU Timeline Card.

On this page you can see all MCU titles timline and sort it by story or release.

By default titles sort by Story

You can simply choose another sorting by press Sort by section.

Story will sort all titles by Main Story timline. Release will sort all titles by projects release date.

If you want hide projects which not made by Marvel Studios you can press Only Marvel Studios in Sort by menu. This option show only Movies, One-Shots and Disney+ TV Shows made by Marvel Studios. This options works for all sorts types. To show all titles press Sort by and press All titles.
