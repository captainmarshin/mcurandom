Help & About

Hi! 👋
Thank you for visiting MCU Random.
I am a big fan of Marvel Cinematic Universe and like others, who like this Universe, often rewatch all projects. On one day I couldn't choose what to watch today? I was search in Google fast random from all MCU, but not found anything interesting. So i decide to make a complete randomiser from all MCU projects. It was hard, because i’m not professional web designer, but with Google and tenacity, after a few weeks, I present you: MCU Random - Get random title from Marvel Cinematic Universe! This random take part from 23 movies, 13 TV shows (include episodes), 6 one-shots and 5 web series. And as a bonus you can make random from movies, which not join in MCU, starts from 1998 Blade movie. Over time and the release of new episodes and projects I’ll add this in Random. On site launch moment (and writing this text) last project is The Falcon and The Winter Soldier (Season 1, Episode 6: One World, One People).

MCU Random is very simple site in functionality and code. Here i show you how to use it and little user tips.

QuickRandom

On the top right of app you can see Quick Random. It’s shows you random title from all MCU titles (exclude episodes), so “In one click” you can get random title. If you press on this element - it’s shows you new title. Work like a watch. P.S And here you can find Easter Egg 🐣

Result window

When you press on Card - it's show you result instantly.

You can press on Random button to show new result, Close button to close window.

To close window you can press Esc on keyboard. Press on Poster image to open current title on IMDb.

Light and Dark Themes

site is support System Appearance, so it have 2 themes: Light and Dark

You don’t need to switch themes on site, colors automatically changes, when you change system appearance.
